# Style Checklist

Use this checklist before delivering any draft or review.

## Facts

- [ ] Fact list created before drafting
- [ ] Core facts confirmed or marked pending
- [ ] No invented quotes, people, organizations, numbers, or schedules
- [ ] Data and dates have sources
- [ ] Conflicts are listed instead of guessed away

## Structure

- [ ] Headline is concise and factual
- [ ] Lead contains the central news point
- [ ] Event facts come before background
- [ ] Paragraphs are short and purposeful
- [ ] Ending is sourced and not slogan-like

## Style

- [ ] Tone is positive but restrained
- [ ] Promotional adjectives are reduced
- [ ] Chinese officialese is removed or rewritten
- [ ] AI-generic phrases are replaced with concrete facts
- [ ] International readers get necessary cultural and geographic context

## Bilingual

- [ ] English and Chinese facts match
- [ ] Names, dates, numbers, quotes, and titles match
- [ ] Pending items appear in both versions
- [ ] The Chinese version is natural news prose, not back-translation

## Non-Imitation

- [ ] No full public article text copied
- [ ] No distinctive paragraph sequence copied from a public article
- [ ] No claim of official China Daily publication or authorization
